About Me

Hi, my name is Jamaal Brown. A self-taught junior software engineer based in Houston, Texas.

Since I was kid I have always been interested in technology. I graduated from the Electrical and Computer Engineering curriculum at UT Austin. I started my career in Power Systems but after deciding that field wasn't for me I started learning full stack software engineering on my own. My love for technology and how it helps us improve drives me to continue learning software in my own time.

Being able to take a step back and really understand the problem that we are trying to solve with what we are creating is really important. Currently in my self learning journey I have worked the main front end technologies such as html, css, and javascript. My backend experience is solely based in SQL at the moment. I do have experience querying an Oracle Database which is with html, css, javascript, and sql. At my current job we use SQL to query an Oracle database quite regularly. Its always a good feeling when you create a query that retrieves and organizes your data efficiently. I enjoy figuring out how to solve problems and learning something new which is what gets me excited about working in the software industry

My favorite things to do in my spare time is yoga, gardening, creating beaded jewelry, traveling and spending time with my friends and family
